Prediction Methodology
Step 1 — Ordering Click Extrapolation
Jan (8.6K) + Feb (9.5K) actual clicks, Mar estimated at 10.3K (+8% MoM conservative). Q1 total: ~28.4K clicks.

Step 2 — Click-to-Test Conversion
Q4 '25 ratio: ~21.5K ordering clicks → 38K tests = 1.77× multiplier. Includes non-web orders (fax, EMR, phone). Applied to Q1: ~50.2K base tests.
Step 3 — Quest EMR Uplift
Quest integration goes live mid-Q1. Conservative +6% partial-quarter boost (400K+ physicians). Adjusted: 48.5K tests.

Step 4 — Confidence Range
±8% band for model uncertainty: 44.6K–52.4K tests. Company guidance: 42.0K–45.0K.
Caveats: Web proxy model uses estimated (not directly measured) portal traffic. Actual conversion rates may differ from Q4. Quest EMR ramp timing is uncertain. ASP mix shift may differ from guidance. This is a directional indicator, not a precise forecast.
